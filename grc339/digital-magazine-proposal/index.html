Introduction

42°

42° is a magazine highlighting the craftsmanship of chairs by industrial designers. It looks at how form is shaped by culture, while still serving its function. The name, drawn from the angular language of design, reflects a perspective committed to objects that define how we sit, gather, and experience space.

Our Story

Welcome to Pressed, where golden mornings meet golden waffles. Inspired by surf culture and the laid-back energy of the coast, we’re all about simple, high-quality ingredients pressed to perfection. Whether you're fueling up for dawn patrol or just chasing a slow morning, our menu is stacked with fresh flavors, strong coffee, and the kind of breakfast that keeps you coming back.

At Pressed, we believe food should feel as good as it tastes. That’s why we keep things local, fresh, and made-to-order—crispy waffles, bold brews, and a lineup of sweet and savory creations designed to hit the spot. No frills, no shortcuts—just honest eats served with a side of good vibes.

But it’s not just about the food—it’s about the experience. The smell of espresso in the air, the sound of a board sliding into the back of a truck, the post-surf warmth of a sunlit seat. We built Pressed to be more than a restaurant—it’s a gathering place for early risers, weekend cruisers, and anyone who knows that the best mornings start with great company and even better breakfast.

So pull up, grab a seat, and take it slow. Whether you're here for a quick bite or a long conversation, we’re stoked to serve up something that fuels the day ahead. Pressed—where waffles, waves, and good mornings come together.
